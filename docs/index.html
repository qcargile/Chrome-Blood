00 · BRIEFING

What is Chrome & Blood

Tuned extensively for Very Hard difficulty, C&B is a full overhaul of Cyberpunk 2077 for players who want Night City to fight back. Combat is lethal, builds are everything, and nothing is handed to you.

500+ mods. Two profiles. Every system — combat, AI, hacking, stealth, economy, cyberware, and vehicles — has been reworked from the ground up.

01 · COMBAT

Combat

Fights in Chrome & Blood last seconds, not minutes. AI flanks, retreats, and calls reinforcements. Every faction fights differently.

  • Faster TTK powered by Enemies of Night City and Chrome Ballistics
  • Faction reinforcements that escalate every fight
  • Damage Scaling and Balance makes every weapon archetype worth building around
02 · BUILDS

Builds

Your chrome defines you. Cyberware has real costs, perks have real impact, and every build plays differently.

  • 18 new perks via Merc Protocol — designed specifically for this list
  • Expanded cyberware through Neuralware and the Cyberware Improved series
  • Realistic slot limits and humanity costs
03 · STEALTH & HACKING

Stealth & Hacking

Stealth is dangerous. Enemies are observant, bodies get found, and one mistake cascades. Hacking has been rebuilt from scratch.

  • Stealth Runner with More Cautious Enemies, More Vigilant Enemies, and Dead Body Alert
  • Much Better Netrunning overhauls hacking from the ground up
  • Ghost a building or watch it all unravel — your call
04 · VISUALS

Night City Reborn

The city looks different. The light hits different. Weather rolls through Night City like it belongs there.

  • ReLUX and Nova City 2 transform the visual foundation
  • HDR LUTs, tuned weather, upgraded fog and LOD
  • Upgraded civilian variety for a city that feels alive
05 · VEHICLES

Vehicles

Reworked handling that actually feels good. A full garage of lore-friendly rides. And nitrous, because why not.

  • Nitrous for that extra push
  • 40+ new vehicles through Virtual Car Dealer
  • Handling reworked by Surface Type Expansion and All vehicles can steer
06 · THE WORLD

The World

Night City feels alive now. Fixers feel like real contacts. Ripperdocs feel like back-alley surgeons. The economy keeps you hungry.

  • Immersive Fixers — gigs feel like actual jobs
  • Ammo limits and bounty hunting add weight to every session
  • Economy rebalanced with a custom Chrome & Blood preset you won't find anywhere else
07 · PROFILES

Choose Your Night City

Chrome & Blood ships with two profiles. Same lethal core, different rules. Pick one in Mod Organizer 2 and hit Run.

Immersive

Hardcore

Survival-focused — built around earning everything

  • Survival, cyberpsychosis, and humanity system via Dark Future
  • HUD elements locked behind cyberware through Immersive Cyberware
  • HUD hidden unless the situation demands it — Limited HUD
  • Deliveries take real time with Virtual Atelier Delivery

Standard

Core

Same reworked combat and systems, no survival layer

  • Full UI and inventory access from the start
  • No cyberpsychosis or humanity system
  • No needs or HUD restrictions
  • Every combat, AI, hacking, and economy change still applies
Cosmetics: as of v3.2, cosmetic mods have been pulled from both profiles. Chrome & Blood is purely gameplay — add whatever cosmetics you want on top, and use the [NoDelete] tag in MO2 to keep your additions safe across updates.

This list isn't meant to be easy. In Chrome & Blood's Night City, survival isn't a right — it's earned.
