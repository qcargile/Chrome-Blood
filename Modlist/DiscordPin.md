# :toolbox: GETTING STARTED WITH CHROME & BLOOD

Everything lives on the website — install guide, the mods, full load order, changelog, FAQ:
:point_right: **https://qcargile.github.io/Chrome-Blood/**

## The short version

1. **Clean install** of Cyberpunk 2077 + Phantom Liberty on Steam — fully updated, outside Program Files, no leftover mods.
2. **Work through the install guide:** https://qcargile.github.io/Chrome-Blood/install.html
   Don't skip the preinstallation steps — that's where most install problems come from.
3. **In Wabbajack**, filter by Cyberpunk 2077 — Chrome & Blood shows under **Featured**. No NSFW or Non-Featured ticks needed.
4. **Only launch through MO2.** Pick **Immersive** or **Standard**, hit Run.

## Before you ask for help

- Check the FAQ: https://qcargile.github.io/Chrome-Blood/faq.html
- Added or removed mods yourself? Read Rule 11 first: https://qcargile.github.io/Chrome-Blood/mods.html#rule11
- Then post what you did and what happened — screenshots and logs help.
